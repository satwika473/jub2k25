Experience the Perfect Blend of Technology, Culture, and Sports!

About Jubilation Fest

Welcome to Jubilation Fest 2025—a grand celebration of technology, management, sports, and culture! This two-day extravaganza brings together the brightest minds, creative talents, and competitive spirits under one roof.

Event Timeline

Day 1: Techno-Management Events

  • 9:00 AM - Inauguration Ceremony

    Grand opening with a welcome speech and lighting of the lamp.

  • 10:00 AM - Technical Events

    Competitions for CSE, ECE, Mechanical, and Civil branches.

  • 2:00 PM - Cultural Programs

    Dance, music, drama, and creative performances by students.

  • 6:00 PM - Closing Ceremony

    Day 1 concludes with a summary and prize distribution.

Day 2: Sports Events

  • 8:30 AM - Assembly & Warm-Up

    Participants gather for morning warm-up and event briefing.

  • 9:00 AM - Athletics

    Track and field events kick off the sports day.

  • 11:00 AM - Team Sports

    Cricket, football, and basketball matches begin.

  • 3:00 PM - Final Matches

    Championship rounds for team sports and relays.

  • 5:30 PM - Closing Ceremony & Awards

    Winners are celebrated, and the fest concludes with a speech.
